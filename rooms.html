Summary of our Facilities

Welcome to Couples Resort, Haliburton’s exclusive 5-star retreat where luxury and wilderness converge. Revel in a daily culinary journey, as all room prices include a 5-course dinner and breakfast, setting the stage for a romantic escape. Surpassing 5 Diamond/Star standards, our accommodations offer an unparalleled experience with private outdoor hot tubs, lavish king-size beds, and balconies or decks with serene lakeside views.

Modern comforts include fiber-optic internet, a 50-inch TV, and a wood-burning fireplace. Whether in a 2-person whirlpool bath tub or on the balcony with chaise lounge chairs, each room is designed for comfort and romance. A proud Canadian-owned establishment for over 60 years, Couples Resort invites you to embrace luxury and natural charm during your unforgettable retreat.
